About Us

Enron Corporation is an American energy, commodities, and services company based in Houston, Texas. It was founded by Kenneth Lay in 1985 as a merger between Lay's Houston Natural Gas and InterNorth, both relatively small regional companies.Enron employs approximately 29,000 staff and is a major electricity, natural gas, communications, and pulp and paper company with revenues of nearly $101 billion. Fortune named Enron "America's Most Innovative Company" for six consecutive years.
